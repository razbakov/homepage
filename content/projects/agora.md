Agora is an experiment in collective governance — a place to explore how groups can make decisions together through trust circles and delegated voting rather than rigid majority rule.

**Problem:** Traditional voting forces a choice between direct democracy (everyone votes on everything, which doesn't scale) and representative democracy (you delegate everything to one person for years). Neither fits how people actually build and share trust.

**Solution:** A liquid-democracy model where you can vote directly on the topics you care about and delegate the rest to people you trust — per topic, revocable at any time. Agora is the working ground for testing what trustable, fluid collective decision-making could look like.
