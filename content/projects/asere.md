Asere (Cuban Study) is a study app for learning Cuban Spanish — the dialect actually spoken on the street in Havana, with its own slang and idioms. Learners create an account and pick up their practice where they left off.

**Problem:** Standard Spanish courses teach a neutral, textbook dialect. They leave learners lost the moment they hit Cuban slang, dropped consonants, and everyday idioms — the way people really talk in Cuba.

**Solution:** A focused web app built around Cuban Spanish specifically. It gives learners a personal account to track progress across sessions and study the vocabulary and expressions that standard courses skip, so they can follow real Cuban conversation.
