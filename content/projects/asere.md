Asere Que Bola is a Cuban catering and foodtruck business in Munich, serving authentic Cuban streetfood for weddings, birthdays, and business events. I designed and built its website so the client could present the brand and take catering enquiries in one place.

**Problem:** Catering options for a private feast or a company event are scattered across social media pages, review sites, and word of mouth. A small foodtruck business had no clean, bookable web presence to show its menu, explain its catering packages, and turn interest into enquiries.

**Solution:** A video-hero Nuxt site that puts the food front and centre — a menu of Cuban dishes, catering packages for private and business events, upcoming foodtruck dates, and a clear "request a quote" call to action. Built with shadcn-vue and TailwindCSS for a warm, modern look that carries the Caribbean feel while staying fast and easy for the client to maintain.
