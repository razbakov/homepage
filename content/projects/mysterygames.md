MysteryGames runs social deduction game nights in Munich, centered on classic Mafia. It's a recurring event brand that brings strangers together around a table for an evening of bluffing, reading people, and having fun.

**Problem:** Meeting new people in a new city is hard, and most social events are either loud bars or awkward small talk. There's little that gives people a structured, playful reason to interact.

**Solution:** Themed Mafia game nights where the game itself does the social work. The website handles the brand, event listings, and waitlist — turning a living-room party game into a repeatable community event people come back to.
