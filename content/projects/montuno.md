Montuno Club is a Munich-based community for salsa and Cuban dance. The website is the home for classes, socials, and everything that keeps the local Cuban-dance scene connected.

**Problem:** Munich's Cuban-dance community lives across scattered WhatsApp groups and Instagram stories, with no central place to find classes, socials, or how to join.

**Solution:** A clean Nuxt site that gives the community a single front door — class information, community identity, and a way for newcomers to get involved. It turns an informal scene into something people can actually find and join.
